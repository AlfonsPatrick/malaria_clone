Frequently Asked Questions

What is malaria?

Malaria is a life-threatening disease caused by parasites that are transmitted to people through the bites of infected mosquitoes.

How does the detection system work?

Our detection system uses advanced image analysis techniques to identify malaria parasites in blood samples, helping healthcare professionals diagnose the disease faster and more accurately.

Can I contribute to the project?

Yes! We welcome contributions from researchers, developers, and volunteers. You can contribute by helping with research, coding, or spreading awareness about the project.
